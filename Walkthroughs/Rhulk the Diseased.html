Rhulk the Diseased – Boss Guide (Arathi Highlands)

Rot-themed attrition fight with periodic AoEs, tank busters, add waves, and a final enrage check.

Overview

Location Arathi Highlands

Encounter type Sustained raid damage, tank management, add control at health thresholds

Difficulty Moderate–High

This encounter revolves around predictable timers (30–60s AoEs and tank hits) and add waves at 50% and 10%. The final burn is the danger zone: adds + enrage + overlapping AoEs.

Abilities

Core mechanics

  • Foul Odor — Spell 7667 Permanent aura on the boss; expect background pressure near melee.
  • Black Rot — Spell 16448 Applied once at pull to the tank and one random non-tank; disease-type debuff.
  • Necrotic Strike — Spell 60626 Every 20s on tank; healing reduction/physical threat — plan active mitigation.
  • Ferocious Enrage — Spell 52400 At 10% HP; damage ramps up significantly — time raid cooldowns and burn.

Periodic abilities

  • Putrid Bile — Spell 16575 Every 45s on the tank; sustained DoT/rot pressure.
  • Plague Stench — Spell 71161 Every 30s raid AoE; comes with a 5s warning yell — pre-empt defensives.
  • Putrid Stench — Spell 12946 Every 50s; AoE pulse centered on the boss — watch melee positioning.
  • Bile Vomit — Spell 59018 Every 60s; large AoE/cone-style effect — face away from raid.

Add summons

  • Summon Bile Slimes — Spell 16865 Triggers at 50% and again at 10% HP; adds must be controlled and burned quickly.

Phases and thresholds

HP Threshold Events Notes
75% Flavor yell Tempo continues — no mechanic change; keep timers rolling.
50% Summon Bile Slimes Add wave; tank damage continues — assign kicks/CC and swap to adds.
25% Flavor yell Use this window to stabilize and prep cooldowns for 10%.
10% Summon Bile Slimes + Ferocious Enrage Final burn; layer raid CDs, kill adds fast, and commit personal defensives.

Strategy

Tanks

  • Mitigate tank busters: Plan cooldowns for Necrotic Strike and Putrid Bile.
  • Control facing: Turn boss away for Bile Vomit to avoid cleaving the raid.
  • Positioning: Keep the boss slightly off the group to reduce Foul Odor impact on non-melee.

Healers

  • AoE prep: Time throughput CDs around Plague Stench (30s) and Bile Vomit (60s).
  • Early triage: Spot-heal Black Rot targets and stabilize the tank after Necrotic Strike.
  • Final burn: Layer externals at 10% during Ferocious Enrage + add wave.

DPS

  • Add priority: Swap immediately to Bile Slimes at 50% and 10%; control with stuns/slows.
  • Burst timing: Save cooldowns for 10% to push through Enrage.
  • Safe positioning: Maintain spread during Plague Stench; avoid boss frontal during Bile Vomit.

Raid

  • Use warnings: React to the 5s yell before Plague Stench — pre-position and pop personals.
  • Cooldown plan: Assign raid CDs to 50% adds and the 10% overlap; consider Lust/Heroism at 10%.
  • Mechanic discipline: Keep melee tight but safe; ranged loosely spread to limit splash.

TL;DR cheat sheet

  • Tanks: CDs for Necrotic Strike and Putrid Bile; face away for Bile Vomit.
  • Healers: Prep raid CDs around 30s/60s AoEs; triage Black Rot targets.
  • DPS: Kill Bile Slimes first at 50% & 10%; save burst for Enrage.
  • Raid: Use the 5s warning before Plague Stench; coordinate Lust/Heroism for the final burn.
